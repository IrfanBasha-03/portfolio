Water body segmentation is crucial for environmental monitoring, flood prediction, and water resource management. This project introduces an advanced deep learning approach using SegFormer, a transformer-based model, for accurate semantic segmentation of water bodies from Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2 satellite imagery. Unlike traditional U-Net models, SegFormer enhances scalability, real-time inference, and generalization across diverse terrains.

AWS Cloud Cost Optimization

The Lambda function fetches all EBS snapshots owned by the same account ('self') and also retrieves a list of active EC2 instances (running and stopped).
